# BizAI for Partners Overview

**Status:** Published (2026-06-30)

## What is BizAI for Partners?

BizAI for Partners is Gupshup's strategy to deliver an agentic AI layer on top of existing Partner APIs, enabling partners to build, resell, and embed autonomous WhatsApp Business agents without learning a new platform.

The strategy rests on two pillars:

1. **Meta-parity plus value-add** — Mirroring Meta's BizAI API surface to provide a familiar contract while layering differentiated capabilities that Meta's raw APIs don't offer.

2. **Partner leverage** — Partners can build once and deploy to multiple channels, maximize their existing Gupshup investment, and unlock new revenue by reselling BizAI to their own customer base.

## How is BizAI Delivered?

Gupshup's partner ecosystem is built on mature Partner APIs that drive WhatsApp Business messaging at scale. Partners have already absorbed the cost of integrating Gupshup's authentication, onboarding flow, and message-handling model.

BizAI is delivered as **new endpoints on the existing Partner API** — not as a separate platform — meaning partners can adopt BizAI without migrating off their existing integration. This reduces adoption friction and allows partners to incrementally add AI capabilities to their existing applications.

## Key Benefits

- **Zero migration required** — Use existing Partner API integration
- **Incremental adoption** — Add AI capabilities at your own pace
- **Multi-channel ready** — Deploy agents across WhatsApp, SMS, email, web
- **Resale-ready** — Bundle and resell to your customers with custom pricing
