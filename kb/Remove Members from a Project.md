# Remove Members from a Project

Managing members of a project can be a challenging task. At times, it may become necessary to delete a member from your project due to various reasons, such as employee termination, resignation, or simply cleaning up your records.

### Steps to Remove a Member:
1. Log in as a Project Admin.
2. Navigate to "Manage Project" on the left navigation bar.
3. Go to the "Members" tab.
4. Locate the member and click the "Remove" or "X" icon.
5. Confirm the removal.
