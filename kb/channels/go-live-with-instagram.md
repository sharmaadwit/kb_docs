### Log in to Instagram

- Log in with your Instagram credentials in the new window.
- After logging in, you might be prompted to save your login info. You can select an option as per your preferences. If you are not sure about what to select, click Not Now.
- If you are not sure about what to select, click Not Now.
### Allow permissions

- Ensure that all permissions are selected (ticked) and click Allow.

### Once you go live, all journeys in the Bot Studio will be active on your Instagram DM.

You can differentiate journeys between channels by following the guide here: Handling Multiple Channels.

### By default, autoresponders are enabled once you go live with Instagram.

If you wish to disable them, you have to follow the steps mentioned in the guide below if you wish to disable them: Customizing/Disabling Autoresponders.
