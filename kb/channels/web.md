## About

- You can now integrate a chat widget on your website with our new Web channel.
- The channel is integrated with our Bot Studio and Agent Assist modules to provide a seamless experience.

### Preview

- A preview of the look & feel of your Web chat widget is shown on the right.
- Any changes you make to the Chat Aesthetics Settings are reflected in the preview.
### Settings

- You can configure your Web chat widget as per your preferences via Settings.
### Testing your Web chat widget

- On clicking the Test button, you are redirected to your dedicated testing page.
- This page mimics the behavior of the Web chat widget that would be observed once it is integrated in your website.
