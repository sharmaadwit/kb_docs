## Still logging in with Facebook?

If you are prompted to log in with your Facebook account while linking your Instagram account with Gupshup, you will also need:

- A Facebook Page that is connected to that Instagram account
- Enabled Allow access to Instagram messages in Inbox for your Instagram account
- Registered as a "Facebook Developer" on the Facebook Developers Portal
### Connecting your Facebook Page and your Instagram business (Professional) account

- Log in to Facebook, then click your profile photo in the top right.
- Click See all profiles, then select the Page that you want to switch into.
- Click your Page's profile picture in the top right of Facebook.
- Click Settings and privacy, then click Settings.
- Under Permissions, click Linked accounts.
- Next to Instagram, click View.
- Click Connect account, then follow the steps to connect your Facebook Page and Instagram account.
- Please ensure that Allow access to Instagram messages in Inbox is enabled in the process.
Source: https://www.facebook.com/business/help/connect-instagram-to-page

### Enabling "Allow access to Instagram messages in Inbox"

If you haven't already done this while connecting your Facebook Page:

- Log in to Facebook, then click your profile photo in the top right.
- Click See all profiles, then select the Page that you want to switch into.
- Click your Page's profile picture in the top right of Facebook.
- Click Settings and privacy, then click Settings.
- Under Permissions, click Linked accounts.
- Next to Instagram, click View.
- Turn on the toggle next to Allow access to Instagram messages in Inbox.
#### Unable to turn on the toggle from Facebook?

- Open the Instagram app on your mobile device and log in to your business (Professional) account.
- Go to your profile and tap the hamburger icon in the top right.
- Select Messages and story replies.
- Select Message controls.
- Under Connected tools, you can toggle on Allow access to messages.
### Registering as a Facebook Developer

- While logged into your Facebook account, go to the Facebook for Developers website and click Get Started.
- Agree to the Terms and Conditions.
- Verify your account using a confirmation code sent to the phone number and email address.
- Select your occupation. You are now registered as a Facebook Developer.
