# Content Training

The Content Training feature allows users to upload and train content from different sources, including website URLs, documents, and raw text. The trained content is used by the ACE LLM to provide accurate and relevant responses to user queries.

Key Features:
- Multiple Content Sources: Upload from website URLs, product catalog documents (PDF, Word, etc.), or input raw text.
- Content Tagging: Organize and categorize content using custom tags.
- Untrain Outdated Content: Remove outdated content from training data.
- Content Audit: Audit training in the Monitoring tab.

Supported Content Sources:
- Website URLs
- Catalog (Product catalogs)
- Documents (PDF, Word, Text files)
- Raw Text
