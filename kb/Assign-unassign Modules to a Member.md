# Assign/unassign Modules to a Member

Let us walk through the steps required to assign modules to members within a project.

Step 1: Login as Project Admin on the Console
Step 2: Navigate to the Members Tab
Step 3: Search for the Member and Click on Assign Modules
Step 4: Select the Modules to Assign
Step 5: Assign Modules

Unassign a Module to a Member:
1. Click on the 'Manage Modules' CTA in the "Modules" column.
2. Unselect the modules that needs to be removed.
3. Click on "Update Modules" button.
