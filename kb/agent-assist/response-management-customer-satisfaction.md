1.1: Conditional Questions in Feedback Form

Definition: Conditional Questions allow the brand to ask follow-up questions based on the feedback provided, such as thumbs, emojis, or stars.

Uses: Conditional Questions enhance feedback collection by seeking detailed responses based on initial feedback.

Steps to Configure Conditional Questions:

Step 1: Create a feedback form and select the condition checkbox.

Step 2: Configure Condition 1 and Condition 2 with questions and options.

Step 3: Map conditions with thumbs ratings, emojis, or stars.

Step 4: Save the configuration.
