## Chat Inbox Structure

Our chat inbox is divided into three sections, offering unparalleled ease-of-use while providing all the features your agents need to deliver top-notch customer support.

Agents can easily switch between different views to see chats that are relevant to them. By clicking on the view name, agents can quickly and easily access chats in that view, saving them time and effort.

This is where agents will spend most of their time. Here, they can:

- Quickly type out responses to customers
- Choose from a library of canned responses to speed up workflow
- Add private notes for internal communication
- Apply tags for better organization
- Change chat priority to ensure efficient handling
Agents can view comprehensive customer information, including chat fields and customer fields. This makes it easy to quickly access customer data, allowing agents to personalize their responses and provide the best possible support.

- Personalized support through customer data integration
