# User Management: Business Hours

**Module**: Agent Assist

## Definition
Business hours allow you to set working hours for teams of agents so the system can assign chats based on those hours.

When a brand is established, default business hours are created automatically:

- `24 hours a day, 7 days a week`
- pre-mapped to the default team

## Steps
1. Go to `Settings`.
2. Click `Business Hours`.
3. Click `Add New`.
4. Configure the business hours, including naming, time zone, and daily hours.
5. Add holidays if those days should be treated as non-working hours.

## What This Controls
- Team working hours
- Time zone
- Daily hours
- Holidays / non-working hours

## Default Behavior
- Default business hours are created when the brand is established.
- Default business hours are pre-mapped to the default team.
- This allows chats to begin getting assigned to agents as soon as agents are added.

## Related Use
- The source states that business hours are used for chat assignment.
- For offline responses that vary by business hours, see `kb/agent-assist/response-management-auto-replies-and-customer-satisfaction.md`.
