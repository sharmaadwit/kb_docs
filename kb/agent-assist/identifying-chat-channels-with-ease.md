Channel Identification Simplified

In the chat profile, you'll find a convenient feature that allows you to identify the specific channel from which the chat originates. Currently, our Console supports the following channels:

1. WhatsApp

- Channel Logo: WhatsApp
2. Instagram

- Channel Logo: Instagram
3. WebApp

- Channel Logo: WebApp
This visual representation of the channel's logo provides immediate clarity on the source of the chat, enabling efficient and effective communication management.
