## Summary
A general organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 10% — Lower support cost
- 45% — Calls deflected to chatbot
- 90% — CSAT rating

## What they did
- One of the fastest growing e-commerce company is enjoying a 100% YoY growth in their loyal customer base
- A growing expectation from its GenZ audience is chat based support. This expectation, coupled with heavy volumes of 70,000-90,000 calls per day, prompted the company to reassess its contact centre costs
- The online shopping company worked with Gupshup to develop a chatbot that delivered swift responses to service queries, FAQs, and complaints. They saw a healthy 40-45% deflection to self-serve, reducing contact center costs by 10% (over Rs. 10 crores). The bot currently handles over 2 mn sessions/month
- With the capability to seamlessly transfer bot conversations to a live agent using Gupshup Agent Assist, the company also seamlessly handles high-risk or complex scenarios
- Following the adoption of the bot and the smooth transition to agent support, the brand saw an impressive CSAT rating of 90%
