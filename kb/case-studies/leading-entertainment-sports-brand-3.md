## Summary
A entertainment organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 82.7% — Journey completion
- 60% — Lead to qualified lead

## What they did
- The organization was looking for novel ideas to increase app downloads in addition to traditional digital advertising
- The brand decided to leverage conversations to influence new leads to download the mobile app, using Gupshup Conversation Cloud
- Click to WhatsApp ads with micro journeys introduced prospects to various offers and prizes they could win by playing on the mobile app
- The brand saw a journey completion rate of 82.7% during the Cricket World Cup and 60% lead to qualified lead conversions at cost per lead of INR 20 Fantasy sports company leverages 2-way conversations to drive app downloads
