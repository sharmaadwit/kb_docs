A financial services organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 2.5X — Qualified leads
- 60% — Lower cost per
- 50% — Lower cost per lead with ads

## What they did
- The organization Capital wanted to use WhatsApp as additional channel for lead generation. They wanted to enable the channel for users to connect with them at their own convenience
- The organization Capital leveraged Click to WhatsApp Ads to promote their Auto Loans. When clicked, the ads took people to an interactive lead qualification conversational journey, where they could apply for loans based on their requirements
- With Click to WhatsApp Ads, the brand was able to acquire new customers in a convenient manager; and helped them apply for loan, book a test drive, buy a new two wheeler and talk to a customer service agent over one-on-one conversation
