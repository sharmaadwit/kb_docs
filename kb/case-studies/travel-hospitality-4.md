## What they did
- The Flows integration aimed to tap into markets traditionally challenging to reach, especially in tier 2-3 regions, showing promising early results with 17,000 users and positive feedback.
- Early feedback indicated a positive user experience, with keywords such as easy, convenient, user-friendly, and simple, showcasing the potential for growth in the business through this innovative channel.
- Using the flow, users can now easily select their departure and destination cities, boarding and dropping points, and journey date with a few taps, streamlining the ticket booking experience.
