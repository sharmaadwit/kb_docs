## What they did
- MTV & MTV Beats, home to hit shows like Hustle and Roadies, faced a new challenge—keeping viewers hooked in a world with expanding content options. They partnered with Gupshup to bring interactive, gamified contests to life, offering real-time engagement.
- By simply scanning a QR code on their TV screens, viewers could instantly participate in contests through WhatsApp.
- Contests like Selfie Le Le Re, where fans saw their selfies on TV, What The Ishq!, where heartfelt messages to loved ones were broadcast, and Vh1 Playlist, where fans’ favorite songs got airtime, became instant hits.
- The results were impressive: 1.87 million messages exchanged, with 47.3K engaged users—79.3% of them unique.
- 26% of users returned for more contests, with each participant averaging 99 message exchanges.
