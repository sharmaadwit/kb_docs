## Key results
- 40% — Business from repeat
- 55% — Share of Direct Orders
- 2.9 mn — Opted in

## What they did
- To make the ordering experience convenient for customers, Wow! Momo introduced WhatsApp as a channel in mid 2022
- The chain engages it 2.9 million opted-in users on WhatsApp by sending the right offer to the right customer on WhatsApp
- To acquire new customers, the brand also uses location based ads on third party platforms, encouraging them to visit the nearby store and place an order over WhatsApp. A whopping 55% of their orders are directly on WhatsApp & in outlets
- To drive repeat orders, Wow! Momo implemented conversational commerce on WhatsApp. This allows customers to view the menu of their nearest store, place an order, make payments and track delivery —all from one platform.
- The chain enjoys an impressive 40% repeat order rate, reducing the burden on new customer acquisition for growth. Leading Indian fast food chain delivers a wow customer experience with end-to-end food ordering on WhatsApp
