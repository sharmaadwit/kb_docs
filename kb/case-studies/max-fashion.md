## What they did
- Max Fashion, part of Landmark Group Middle East aimed to unify customer experience across offline and online, drive repeat purchases, and boost cross-category sales - both year-round and during key events.
- The value fashion brand created campaigns for shopper segments like new app users, dormant customers, high-value buyers; using behavior, language, and shopping data.
- Successful campaigns include “Suhoor Surprises” during late Ramadan hours, womenswear promos for moms buying kidswear, post-purchase rewards to encourage repeat buys, and event-driven offers for Back to School and White Wednesday.
