## Outcome
Leading retail / D2C brand in India — Cost per lead Supermarket chain leverages CTWA to acquire new customers and drive app downloads, expanding to 3 cities in 2 months

## Summary
A retail & d2c organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 13% — Converted to leads

## What they did
- The organization, a one-stop supermarket chain with presence in 10 states across India was looking for a novel approach to acquire new customers and drive app downloads
- The grocer routed its social ads to their WhatsApp Business handle, helping customers to browse the offers flyer and discounts
- Customers were then encouraged to download the The organization mobile app and continue shopping to avail the offers
- With an impressive cost per link click of INR 1.6, The organization was able to generate 41K leads in 2 months
- 13% of those who clicked on the ad CTA button converted into a lead with cost per lead of INR 12.4 41K+ Leads in 2 months
