## What they did
- Enabled The organization to collect new rider information for automated onboarding such as name, contact, area of work, shift and timing preferences via WhatsApp chatbot
- Chatbot keeps track of onboarding completion status for each user and allows user to review and edit their details later
- After completion, rider is redirected to download The organization rider app from play store
- Regular nudges for riders who didn’t complete onboarding process via WhatsApp notifications
