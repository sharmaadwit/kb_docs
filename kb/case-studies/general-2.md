## Summary
A general organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## What they did
- Providing demand based and industry relevant skills to the youth, TNSDC aims to improve employability and transform the state into a skill hub
- TNSDC deployed WhatsApp handle to enable applicants interact with the chatbot in English or Tamil, and get details such as location, qualification, salary range and experience
- Job seeker gets option to browse jobs from a list of 43 industries and can set salary filters
- Candidate can also raise complaints around misconduct by stakeholders involved Tamil Nadu Skill Development Corporation implements a job search assistant to help applicants find new openings based on their experience and needs 200+ Daily users coming to chatbot 10+ Messages per session 10L+ Users Targeted through Campaigns
