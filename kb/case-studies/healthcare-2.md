## Summary
A healthcare organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 23K — Leads generated
- 16K — Qualified leads generated

## What they did
- The organization, a specialist medical center in UAE offering dental, cosmetic, laser and skincare solutions wanted to boost its appointment bookings
- The medical complex partnered with Gupshup to launch Click to WhatsApp Ads and leveraged live agent conversations to assist customers
- Prospects, after seeing the ad, could connect with the medical center’s agents over WhatsApp, share their concerns and schedule an appointment for their preferred time
- The organization generate 23K leads and 16K qualified leads within 45 days of going live with cost per lead of AED 30.6
