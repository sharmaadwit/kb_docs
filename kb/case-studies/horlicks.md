# Horlicks worked with Gupshup to adopt WhatsApp

## Summary
A cpg organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## What they did
- Horlicks, the popular consumer healthcare brand, wanted to explore conversational channel to book diet consultation and diabetes risk test appointments and share customized diet plans
- With its ability to deliver rich media and interactive options, WhatsApp was the channel of choice
- Horlicks worked with Gupshup to adopt WhatsApp to help customers book appointment for counselling or diabetes tests
- The brand saw a considerable increase in user interaction with over 1000 users booking consultation appointment on a daily basis Horlicks, the popular nutritional drink boosts engagement and improves appointment booking using bots over WhatsApp
