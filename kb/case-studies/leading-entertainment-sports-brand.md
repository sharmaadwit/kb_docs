## Summary
A entertainment organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 61.9% — Qualified leads
- 90% — Quality leads, building
- 2X — ROAS

## What they did
- The organization, a new age sports streaming, content, analytics and experiences brand wanted to generate qualified leads for its live sport travel assistance service
- The brand partnered with Gupshup to run click to WhatsApp ads
- With sponsored posts on Facebook & Instagram the brand prompted users to chat with them on WhatsApp to book tickets for the sporting event
- With a compelling ad creative and clear CTA setting expectations of WhatsApp chat, the brand was able to generate 263 leads in 39 days
- The engaging micro-journey saw ~ 62% journey completion rate as the bot qualified 163 leads, understanding their travel readiness and experience preferences
- The brand’s sales team could then efficiently convert these qualifying leads, seeing over 2X return on ad spend
- Created a strong first-party database with 90% of the leads being high-intent
