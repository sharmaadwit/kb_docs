# Unilever’s eco-friendly beauty brand builds brand awareness and boosts conversions with WhatsApp based shopping and order management

**Company**: Leading enterprise in its sector

## Summary
A general organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 3X — Orders placed
- 2X — User interaction

## What they did
- Love beauty and planet partnered with Gupshup to send promotional messages like holiday offers, discounted prices and help them reach a wider audience for their new products
- The need - a reliable messaging solution that also integrated with LimeChat
- Gupshup’s conversational commerce capabilities helped showcase their products across categories - skincare, bodycare, bestsellers, allowing customers to purchase over WhatsApp
- The brand saw 2X more user interaction and 3X orders placed
