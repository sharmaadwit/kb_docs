## Summary
A retail & d2c organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 4X — Engagement
- 81.8% — Higher open rates

## What they did
- Looking for new ways to engage shoppers and grow revenues, Reserva adopted WhatsApp for segment based promotions, cross-sell and nudges. These targeted campaigns saw a high CTR of 40%, (a big jump from 10% on SMS and 2.2% on email)
- Recognizing the value of conversations, Reserva partnered with Gupshup to launch a Gen AI-powered shopping assistant for their Mother’s Day campaign
- The personal shopping assistant not just helps customers with product recommendations, but asks questions to understand the style preferences and consultatively helps them select the perfect Mother’s Day present
