## What they did
- The organization, a popular omnichannel home improvement brand in Southeast Asia was looking for new ways to drive commerce
- The brand launched Click to WhatsApp ads connected to an interactive automated journey
- After landing on the WhatsApp account of the brand from the ad, new prospects could browse products, add to cart & complete the purchase conversationally
- The brand generated 1,327 leads in 27 days at cost per lead of IDR 31,884
- 19% of users who clicked on the ad converted into leads 1,327 Leads in 27 days IDR 31K Cost per Lead Leading omnichannel home goods brand leverages CTWA to drive commerce in Indonesia
