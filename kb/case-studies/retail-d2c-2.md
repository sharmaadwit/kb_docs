## Summary
A retail & d2c organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 56% — Lead to qualified leads
- 75% — Reduction in Cost Per Qualified leads

## What they did
- The organization - a comfort tech company selling mattresses and furniture wanted to up their first party database and store visits
- In partnership with Gupshup, the brand designed two CTWA ad campaigns; one for Pillow exchange and another for Black Friday sale
- Recognizing that high-ticket size products require store visits and more sharing, the brand encouraged customers to visit a store
- Through automated conversations the brand assessed prospect’s interest & shared a coupon followed by navigation to the nearest store for redemption
- The campaign helped the brand generate 8,000 leads and 4,500 qualified leads within just first 5 weeks
- The innovative approach reduced the cost for qualified leads by 75% compared to traditional digital channels Automated free retargeting in 72 hours
