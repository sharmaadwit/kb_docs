## What they did
- The organization, an Indian e-commerce company, sought a novel approach to boost app downloads as part of their marketing calendar
- Launched Click to WhatsApp ad campaign, showcasing ads on Facebook that promoted app downloads
- While the campaign worked, they realized there was scope to reduce the cost per download
- Gupshup recommended the best practice of incentivising and engaging the users with a gamified fun quiz. Based on their score, participants received different amounts of Snapcash, redeemable on the app
- The brand generated saw an 80% qualification rate, and 36.5% conversion rate
- Cost per app downloads was down to 1/10th with gamified journey from CTWA
