## Outcome
Leading retail / D2C brand — Electronics retailer and e-commerce player achieves 4X ROAS with Click to WhatsApp Ads

## Summary
A retail & d2c organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 4X — Return on Advertising
- 34% — Lower Cost Per
- 43% — Goal

## What they did
- The organization wanted to generate high quality leads while increasing conversions during the holy month of Ramadan
- In partnership with Gupshup, the brand launched Click to WhatsApp ads encouraging prospects to click and get a surprise Eid gift
- Users chatted with the bot on WhatsApp and received a coupon code redeemable at online/offline points of sale
- The brand generated 9.5K leads at 34% lower CPL compared to digital ads which directed users to their website
- The campaign delivered 43% leads to qualified leads conversion
- Achieved 4X ROAS; generated AED 146,000 in revenue against ad spend of AED 30,000
