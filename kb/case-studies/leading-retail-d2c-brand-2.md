## Key results
- 10K — Conversations

## What they did
- The organization wanted to recreate in-store like shopping assistance to customers on their digital storefronts
- With Gupshup, The organization built an AI-powered omnichannel virtual assistant, helping customers with product discovery, shopping and post purchase support such as order tracking, cancellation etc
- In 4 months, Angela - the brand’s virtual assistant, helped The organization resolve 70% of customer queries, manage 10K conversations a month, and provide 13K hrs of support without human agent and influenced orders worth INR 40 Lacs
- What started as a website only bot, soon expanded to messaging channels including GBM and WhatsApp
