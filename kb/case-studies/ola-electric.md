## Summary
A automotive organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 155 K — Leads Sep-Oct 2025
- 48% — Lead to QL

## What they did
- Ola Electric used Click-to-Chat Ads to drive high-intent leads for its new product launch.
- The campaign connected users directly on WhatsApp in multiple languages, ensuring a personalized experience across regions.
- The chat ﬂow qualiﬁed leads by asking about vehicle preference (bike/scooter), budget, daily travel distance, and ﬁnancing needs.
- Qualiﬁed prospects received an instant conﬁrmation that the team would follow up with tailored options, streamlining lead conversion.
