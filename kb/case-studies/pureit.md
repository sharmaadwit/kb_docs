## Summary
A cpg organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## Key results
- 2X — Installation requests
- 50% — Lower support calls
- 3X — Product queries

## What they did
- Pureit initially offered its customers the option to inquire, schedule home demonstrations, and raise complaints via phone support.
- Experiencing high growth, they sought a scalable solution and partnered with Gupshup to adopt messaging channels.
- In addition to promotional campaigns, they leveraged interactive bot journeys to allow customers to inquire about products, schedule demo, place an order, raise complaints and chat with live agent - all from WhatsApp.
- To expand reach, Pureit expanded the self-serve journey to channels like RCS, Instagram, and GBM.
- As a result, the number of support calls drastically fell by 50%, with more customers opting to use messaging channels.
