## Summary
A travel & hospitality organization used Gupshup conversational messaging to drive measurable business outcomes.

## What they did
- The organization was looking to improve the quality of first-party data collected from their digital ads. Leads forms often attract dummy phone numbers resulting in wasted ad spend and sales rep effort in follow-ups
- With the new CTA of WhatsApp chat, prospects enter a seamless journey and share their personal details and car preferences. With lead qualification on WhatsApp, sales reps spend their time converting these customers through assisted selling
- The car lease provider is not only able to qualify leads better, but also plans to retarget prospects with relevant offers and nudges on to WhatsApp to achieve more conversions without further ad spends Pre-owned car leasing platform addresses the issue of invalid phone numbers in lead forms; automates lead qualification with WhatsApp journeys 1756 Leads with a CPL of INR 31 INR 592 Cost per qualified lead Lower Sales effort for lead qualification and conversion
