- 54% — Open Rate compared
- 1.8X — Conversions over traditional
- 60% — Feedback

## What they did
- Housing.com relied heavily on social media ads to attract buyers and used emails and push notifications to engage prospects
- Facing reduced response on traditional channels, they teamed up with Gupshup to develop a multi-faceted strategy to drive property discovery
- A unique property recommendation bot was developed, which leverages user data and search history to personalize property suggestions for buyers on WhatsApp
- When the buyer expresses interest, a direct connection is established with the seller, eliminating the need to switch platforms and fast tracking deal progression
- This simplified approach resulted in high adoption with monthly conversations on WhatsApp reaching 8 mn, an open rate of 53% and conversion rates improving by 80%
- By leveraging chatbots, Housing enhanced their buyer feedback collection, achieving a 60% increase, while streamlining the property listing process for sellers via direct photo uploads, and more
