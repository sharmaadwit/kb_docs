## What they did
- Byju’s was facing challenges with their communication channels during the COVID times due to frequent downtime and lack of a monitoring system that could help the managers access agent productivity
- Byju’s required a solution that could allow managers, at multi-hierarchical levels, access to the performance of their agents
- Gupshup helped Byju’s deploy a scalable Click-to-call solution which is being used amongst four of its study verticals. Equipped with a tracking system, the solution allows managers to access agents’ performance in real-time
- With easier access to call recordings, managers can make better knowledgeable decisions when it comes to the agents’ training requirements
