## What they did
- Introduced WhatsApp as a channel for marketing in mid 2022 after seeing success with authentication and OTP use cases on WhatsApp
- In addition to promotional campaigns on Whatsapp, Swiggy took their engagement a notch up with interactive bot journeys
- To reactivate dormant users, the brand sent personalised promotions to the segment, seeing a tremendous 1.5X better reactivation rate over other channels
- With Swiggy DineOut, they launched Dine Buddy allowing users to find Instagram worthy, Nightlife etc. The campaign saw 2.3X higher engagement on whatsapp over email Swiggy grows customer engagement and simplifies food ordering with conversational messaging
