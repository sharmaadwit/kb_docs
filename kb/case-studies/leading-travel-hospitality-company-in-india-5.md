## What they did
- The organization sought to manage agent workload and cut call center costs by transitioning from call-based to chatbot support for post-purchase interactions
- They implemented a post purchase upsell campaign on WhatsApp. This includes nudging customers who booked their tickets for booking SMB (seat, meal, baggage) with a handover to live agent to process the payment. We enabled: ○ Native integration with Salesforce ○ Deep analytics for bot and Agent Assist ○ Ability to build, deploy and scale complex flow quickly
- The organization has seen a 25% higher engagement as compared to SMS on SMB campaigns with customers booking SMB on the go with close to 3% conversion. Additionally WhatsApp messages count has increased by 1-2 messages/customer
- Next phase includes GenAI for assisted selling and interactive campaigns, expanding to another 10 use cases like VIP tag booking flow, check-in, rescheduling etc. Popular travel booking provider grows upsell revenue with targeted campaigns for add-ons
