# Assign/Unassign Projects

### Assigning Projects
1. Log in to your organization's management system and navigate to the page for managing members.
2. Search for the member to whom you want to assign projects.
3. Click on the "Assign Projects" link.
4. Select the projects that you want to assign to the member.
5. Click on the "Add" button.
6. The projects will be assigned to the member, and the member will receive a notification.

### Unassigning Projects
1. Click on the “cross” button of the assigned project to unassign it.
