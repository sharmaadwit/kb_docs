# Before you begin

### Prerequisites for Instagram Integration
Before linking your Instagram account with Gupshup, confirm that you have:
1. A Professional Instagram account (either a Creator or Business account).
2. Your Instagram account login credentials.

### Converting to a Professional Account
1. Go to your Instagram profile and tap the hamburger icon.
2. Select Settings and privacy.
3. For some accounts, the option to "Switch to professional account" will be listed directly under Settings.
4. Tap Account type and tools.
5. Tap Switch to professional account.
6. Follow the prompts to finish setting up your professional account.

### Connecting Facebook Page
Ensure your Facebook Page is connected to your Instagram professional account and "Allow access to Instagram messages in Inbox" is enabled.
