# Add Members

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to add members to a project on Console.

### Step 1: Log in as a Project Admin on Console
To add members to a project, you must first log in as a Project Admin on Console.

### Step 2: Navigate to the Members Tab
Once logged in, click on "Manage Project" and navigate to the "Members" tab.

### Step 3: Click on Add Member
Click on the "Add Member" button.

### Step 4: Provide Member Details
Enter the member's email address and select the required modules or projects to assign to them.

### Step 5: Save
Click on "Save" or "Apply" to add the member to the project.
