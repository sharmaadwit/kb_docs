# Reset Password

### How to Reset Your Password: A Step-by-Step Guide
Follow the steps below to reset your password on Console:
1. Click on "Forgot Password" on the login screen.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Check your email for a reset link.
4. Click the link and enter a new password.
5. Confirm the new password and click "Reset".

### Password Security Tips:
- Use a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols.
- Avoid using easily guessable information like birthdays.
- Change your password periodically.
