# Delete Members of an Organisation

Managing a team or organisation can be a challenging task, especially when it comes to handling member accounts. At times, it may become necessary to delete a member from your organisation.

### Step 1: Access the Manage Organisation Section
Log in to your organisation account and navigate to the management section. Look for the "Manage Organisation" or "Organisation Settings" option.

### Step 2: Locate the Member to be Deleted
Search for the member using their name or email address. Click on the account to select it.

### Step 3: Click on the Delete Icon
Click on the "Delete" icon or button to initiate the process.

### Step 4: Confirm the Deletion
A confirmation popup will appear. Double-check the selection and click "Delete" again.

### Step 5: Member Deleted
The account will no longer be accessible, and associated data will be removed.
