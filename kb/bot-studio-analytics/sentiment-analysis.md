# Sentiment Analysis

**Module**: Bot Studio Analytics

## Definition

Sentiment analysis monitors and evaluates the emotional tone of customer interactions with your bot. This feature is available through the Custom Bot Analytics extension, providing insights into how customers feel about their experience with your bot.

## Procedure

### Exact UI path

Gupshup Console → Bot Studio Analytics → Dashboard → Sentiment Analysis (when Custom Bot Analytics extension is activated)

### Prerequisites

- **Custom Bot Analytics extension** must be activated for your console project
- Access to Gupshup Console → Bot Studio Analytics

### How to access sentiment analysis

1. Contact your Delivery team to activate the Custom Bot Analytics extension for your console project
2. Once the extension is activated, all sentiment analysis data will be automatically visible in Bot Studio Analytics
3. Navigate to Bot Studio Analytics → Dashboard to view sentiment trends and analysis

### What you'll see

Once sentiment analysis is enabled:
- Real-time sentiment metrics for customer interactions
- Sentiment trends over time
- Breakdown by sentiment categories (positive, negative, neutral)
- Ability to drill down into specific conversations

### Troubleshooting

**If you don't see sentiment analysis data:**
- Confirm that the Custom Bot Analytics extension has been activated for your console project
- Contact your Delivery team if the extension is not yet enabled
- Data may take some time to populate after activation (usually within 24 hours)
- Ensure you have appropriate permissions to access Bot Studio Analytics

**Still can't find it?**
- Sentiment analysis requires active conversation data in your bot
- If your bot is new or has low traffic, sentiment data may not be visible yet
- Reach out to support with your project ID and the Delivery team will troubleshoot
