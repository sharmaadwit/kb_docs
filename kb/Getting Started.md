# Welcome to Gupshup Console

Your unified platform for conversational messaging across WhatsApp, SMS, Web, and Instagram. Manage campaigns, bots, and customer support all in one place.

Console is a powerful portal that allows businesses to have multiple accounts of WhatsApp and SMS visible with a single login. The interface is user-friendly and easy to navigate, making it a convenient option for businesses that are looking to streamline their communication channels. With Console, users can select the account they want to use and get started with just a few clicks.

One of the most significant advantages of Console is its ability to integrate with chatbots and agent assist automatically. This means that users can onboard their Waba account and start using chatbots and agent assist features without any additional setup. This makes it a valuable tool for businesses looking to automate their customer support operations and improve their response times.

In addition to managing multiple WhatsApp and SMS accounts, Console also allows businesses to invite new members of their organisation with specific roles and services assigned to them. This can be particularly useful for delegating tasks and responsibilities within an organisation and ensuring that each member has access to the resources they need to do their job effectively.
