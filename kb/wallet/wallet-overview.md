## Step-by-step configuration
You can use your Gupshup wallet to pay for whatsapp and Instagram usage on Converse.

Wallet is supported in INR and USD currencies. For INR wallets the KYC needs to be completed, before recharging the wallet, the KYC can be done using GSTIN number or PAN number for the business.

In this guide we will help you learn how to manage your wallet

## Recharge your wallet

You can recharge your wallet by using the payment gateways available on Gupshup i.e Stripe (USD wallets), Razorpay (INR wallets).

Here's a quick video to guide you on how to recharge your wallet

## Setting low balance alert

You can set low balance alerts, which enable Gupshup to send you emails reminding your to recharge your Gupshup wallet.

Here's a quick video to guide you on how to set the low balance alert

Set low balance alert

## View your wallet usage

You can also view your wallet usage on the wallet home page and upon clicking on the view more page. You can also email the wallet statement to yourself and up to 2 additional email addresses.
