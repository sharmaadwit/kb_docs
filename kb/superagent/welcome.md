# Welcome to SuperAgent

**Module**: SuperAgent

SuperAgent is an AI-powered platform that helps you accomplish real tasks. Chat with an intelligent assistant that can use tools, skills, and integrations to do things for you—from sending emails and analyzing data to scheduling tasks and browsing the web.

## What can you do with SuperAgent?

- [**Agents**: Create specialized AI assistants for specific jobs—research, writing, data analysis, and more.](/documentation/concepts/agents)
- [**Skills**: Add custom capabilities like data analysis, email, spreadsheets, and other tools to your agents.](/documentation/concepts/skills)
- [**Integrations**: Connect Gmail, Jira, Google Sheets, Google Docs, Slack, and other apps so your AI can work with your data.](/documentation/concepts/integrations)
- [**Scheduled Tasks**: Automate recurring work with scheduling and triggers—daily summaries, weekly reports, and more.](/documentation/concepts/scheduled-tasks)
- [**Recipes**: Use pre-built workflow templates to get started quickly with common tasks.](/documentation/concepts/recipes)
- [**Browser Control**: Let agents browse the web, fill forms, and interact with websites on your behalf.](/documentation/concepts/browser-control)
