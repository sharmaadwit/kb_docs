# Getting Started

**Module**: SuperAgent

> [!NOTE]
> **Prerequisites · ~5 minutes**
> You need a Google account to sign in. No installation or technical setup required.

Follow these steps to get up and running with SuperAgent. You'll be chatting with your AI assistant in no time.

## See it in the app

### Step 1: Sign in with Google

Go to SuperAgent and sign in with your Google account. This gives you instant access—no password to remember. Once you're in, you'll see the main chat interface.

### Step 2: Start your first conversation

Just type your question or request in the chat box and press Enter. Try something simple like "What can you help me with?" or "Summarize the key points of productivity." The AI will respond and can use tools to help you get things done.

### Step 3: Try a skill

Skills add extra capabilities to your AI. For example, ask it to "Create a bar chart of my sales data" or "Send an email to my team about the meeting." Open the Skills panel to see what's available and enable the ones you need.

### Step 4: Connect your first integration

Connect Gmail, Google Sheets, Google Docs, Jira, Slack, or another integration so the AI can work with your real data. Go to Integrations, pick a service, and follow the prompts to authorize. Once connected, you can ask things like "Show my unread emails" or "Add a row to my spreadsheet."

### Step 5: Create your first agent

Agents are specialized assistants for specific tasks. Create one for research, writing, data analysis, or customer support. Give it a name, a short description, and pick the skills it needs. Then switch to that agent when you want focused help.
