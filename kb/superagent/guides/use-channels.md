# Use Messaging Channels

**Module**: SuperAgent

> [!NOTE]
> **Prerequisites · ~2 minutes**
> You need a SuperAgent account and WhatsApp or Telegram installed on your phone.

You can chat with SuperAgent from your phone using WhatsApp or Telegram. Set up a channel once, and you'll be able to send messages and get AI responses directly in your messaging app.

## See it in the app

### Step 1: Open Settings from the sidebar

Click **Settings** in the sidebar footer (user area at the bottom). The Settings modal opens.

### Step 2: Click the Channels tab

In the left navigation, click **Channels**. You'll see **WhatsApp** and **Telegram** with a **Connect** button for each.

### Step 3: Click Connect on WhatsApp

Click **Connect** on WhatsApp (or Telegram). The QR code and verification instructions appear.

### Step 4: Scan QR code and send the verification code

Scan the QR code with your phone. Your messaging app opens. Paste the **verification code** shown on screen and send it. Once verified, the channel is linked to your account.

### Step 5: Chat with SuperAgent via WhatsApp

You're all set. Use the **WhatsApp** or **Telegram** button next to Send in the chat bar, or message directly from your phone. Same AI, same skills — from anywhere.

---

## Using your channel

Messages you send in the channel go to the same AI you use on the web. Your conversation history, skills, and integrations apply. You can switch between the web app and your phone seamlessly.

## Disconnecting a channel

To disconnect WhatsApp or Telegram, go back to Settings or Channels and click "Disconnect" for that channel. You can reconnect anytime by following the same setup steps.

> [!TIP]
> **Use both channels**
> You can connect WhatsApp and Telegram at the same time. Use whichever is more convenient — both connect to the same AI with the same skills and integrations.
