# Submit Feedback

**Module**: SuperAgent

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick guide · ~1 minute**
> You need a SuperAgent account to submit or vote on feedback.

SuperAgent includes a built-in feedback system so you can suggest features, report bugs, and see what other users are requesting. Your input helps shape the product roadmap.

## See it in the app

Watch the walkthrough below, then follow the numbered steps for details.

### Step 1: Click Feedback

Click **Feedback** in the left sidebar to open the feedback panel.

### Step 2: Browse feedback

Browse existing suggestions and bug reports. Use the category filters or search to find specific items. Upvote ideas you agree with.

### Step 3: Click New Feedback

Click the **+ New Feedback** button in the top-right corner of the feedback panel to open the submission form.

### Step 4: Submit your feedback

Choose a category (**Feature Request** or **Bug**), enter a title, add a detailed description, and click **Submit Feedback**.

---

## Categories

When submitting feedback, choose one of two categories:

- **Feature Request** — Suggest a new capability or improvement to an existing feature.
- **Bug** — Report something that isn't working as expected, including UI glitches or errors.

## Voting

Each feedback item has an upvote button. Click it to add your vote and help prioritize the most requested features. You can see the total vote count next to each item.

## Status tracking

Feedback items have a status badge that changes as your team reviews them:

- **New** — Just submitted, not yet reviewed
- **In Development** — Actively being worked on
- **Completed** — Shipped and available
- **No Action** — Reviewed but not planned

> [!TIP]
> **Your feedback matters**
> All feedback is visible to other users in your organization. The more votes an item gets, the more likely it is to be prioritized.
