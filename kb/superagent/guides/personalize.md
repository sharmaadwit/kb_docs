# Personalize Responses

**Module**: SuperAgent

> [!NOTE]
> **Prerequisites · ~2 minutes**
> You need a SuperAgent account. No other setup is required.

Personalization lets you tell the AI about your role, preferences, and communication style. The AI uses this information to tailor its responses across all your conversations.

## See it in the app

### Step 1: Open Settings from the sidebar

Click **Settings** in the sidebar footer (user area at the bottom). The Settings modal opens with Profile selected by default.

### Step 2: View the Profile tab

The **Profile** tab has two sections: **More About You** (background, preferences, location) and **Custom Instructions** (how the AI should respond).

### Step 3: Edit custom instructions and save

Fill in your preferences — describe your role, tone, format, and any constraints. Click **Save Changes**. Your instructions apply to all future conversations.

---

## What are custom instructions?

Custom instructions are notes you write about yourself that the AI reads before responding. You can describe your job, how you like to communicate, what level of detail you prefer, and any other context that helps the AI serve you better.

## Examples of good custom instructions

- **Role and context** — "I'm a marketing manager at a B2B SaaS company. I work on campaigns, content, and analytics."
- **Communication style** — "Prefer short, bullet-point answers. Avoid jargon. Use simple language."
- **Preferences** — "Always include action items at the end. Cite sources when making claims."
- **Constraints** — "Don't assume I know technical terms—explain them briefly."

## How it affects your conversations

Your custom instructions apply to every conversation you have with SuperAgent. The AI considers them when answering questions, so you don't need to repeat your preferences each time. Update your instructions whenever your needs change.

> [!INFO]
> **Privacy**
> Your personalization settings are private and only used to improve your experience. They are not shared with other users.
