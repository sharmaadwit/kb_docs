# Frequently Asked Questions

**Module**: SuperAgent

## General

What is SuperAgent?

SuperAgent is an AI-powered platform that helps you automate tasks. You chat with an AI assistant that can use tools, connect to your apps, browse the web, and run complex workflows — all through natural conversation.

Do I need technical knowledge to use SuperAgent?

Not at all. SuperAgent is designed for everyone. You interact with it by typing messages in plain language, just like chatting with a colleague. The AI handles the technical work behind the scenes.

How do I sign in?

Click "Sign In" on the home page and use your Google account. If you can't sign in, check with your organization's administrator to confirm your account is approved.

## Features

What is an Agent?

An Agent is a specialized AI assistant you create for a specific purpose. For example, you might create a "Sales Report Agent" that knows how to pull data from your spreadsheets and generate weekly summaries. Learn more in [Agents](/documentation/concepts/agents).

What is a Skill?

A Skill is a custom capability that extends what the AI can do. Think of it like adding a new tool to the AI's toolbox. Skills can do things like analyze data, send emails, create reports, and more. See [Skills](/documentation/concepts/skills) for details.

What is a Recipe?

A Recipe is a pre-built workflow template. Instead of explaining a complex task from scratch, you use a recipe that already has the steps laid out. It's like following a proven playbook. Learn more about [Recipes](/documentation/concepts/recipes).

What is the difference between a Skill and a Recipe?

A **Skill** is a tool — it gives the AI a specific capability (like reading a spreadsheet). A **Recipe** is a workflow — it tells the AI how to approach a task step by step (like "analyze last month's sales and create a summary report"). Recipes often use multiple skills together.

Which apps can I connect?

SuperAgent integrates with Gmail, Google Sheets, Google Calendar, Google Drive, Google Docs, Jira, Salesforce, Slack, and more. Check the [Integrations](/documentation/concepts/integrations) page for the full list.

## Usage

Can I schedule tasks to run automatically?

Yes. You can set up scheduled tasks that run at specific times (like every morning at 9 AM) or trigger them on demand using a webhook URL. Learn how in [Schedule a Task](/documentation/guides/schedule-task).

Can I use SuperAgent from my phone?

Yes. You can connect SuperAgent to WhatsApp or Telegram and chat with it directly from your phone. See [Use Messaging Channels](/documentation/guides/use-channels) for setup instructions.

Is my data secure?

SuperAgent takes data security seriously. Your conversations and connected integrations are private to your account. Integration authorizations use industry-standard secure protocols, and you can disconnect any integration at any time. If something isn't working as expected, check the [Troubleshooting](/documentation/troubleshooting) page.

Can I share agents or skills with my team?

Yes. Skills have visibility settings that let you share them with specific people or make them available to everyone. Agents are currently personal to your account. See [Manage Skills](/documentation/guides/manage-skills) for details on sharing.
