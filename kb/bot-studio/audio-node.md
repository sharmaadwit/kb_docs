## Introduction

The JB canvas has been introduced with one more node with frequently used use- cases. Audio node, which can be used by businesses to send audio messages to supported channels within the file format & size limitations imposed by the channels. The supported channels of Audio node are: Web, Instagram, WhatsApp, GIP

Audio Node

Supported Formats: aac, mpga, mp2, mp2a, mp3, m2a, m3a, amr, .oga, .ogg, .spx, .opus

## Flexibility of the Node

- File Upload: Bot designers can upload an audio file of a supported format.
- Link a URL/Variable: Bot designers can link a URL or variable that contains the audio file.
- Validation: Uploaded files will be validated for format and size. URLs/Variables will not be validated.

## Use Cases

Here are a few use cases that demonstrate the benefits of the validations added:

### Personalized Birthday Wishes

Scenario: A business wants to send personalized birthday greetings to customers via WhatsApp or Instagram. Benefit: Using the Audio node, the business can deliver heartfelt, customized birthday messages, making customers feel valued and enhancing customer loyalty through a personal touch.

### Reminder Messages

Scenario: A healthcare provider needs to send appointment reminders to patients across multiple platforms. Benefit: The Audio node allows the provider to send clear and friendly voice reminders via Web or WhatsApp, increasing the likelihood that patients will remember and attend their appointments.

### Audio Instructions

Scenario: A tech support team needs to guide users through troubleshooting steps for a common issue. Benefit: The Audio node enables the team to send detailed voice instructions directly to users, making it easier for them to follow along and resolve issues quickly without confusion.

### Order Status Updates

Scenario: A customer has placed an order and is eagerly awaiting updates on its progress, such as confirmation, shipping, and delivery. Benefit: Using the Audio node, the business can send real-time audio updates directly to the customer through channels like WhatsApp or Web. These audio updates provide a more personal and immediate way to keep customers informed, enhancing their overall experience and reducing anxiety about their order status.
