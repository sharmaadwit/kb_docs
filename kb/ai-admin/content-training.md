## Step-by-step configuration
The Content Training feature allows users to upload and train content from different sources, including website URLs, documents, and raw text. The trained content is used by the ACE LLM to provide accurate and relevant responses to user queries.

Key Features: Multiple Content Sources: Upload content from website URLs, product catalog documents (PDF, Word, etc.), or input raw text directly.

Content Tagging: Organize and categorize the content using custom tags.

Untrain Outdated Content: Outdated content can be removed from training data

Content Audit: Content training audit available in Monitoring tab

Supported Content Sources

- Website URLs: Enter a URL to extract and train content from web pages
- Catalog: Upload latest product catalog
- Documents: Upload files such as PDFs, Word documents, or text files for training.
- Raw Text: Add the raw text directly as input
